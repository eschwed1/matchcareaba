NJ's Autism Insurance Mandate Protects Hoboken Families

New Jersey passed its autism insurance mandate in 2009 — one of the first and strongest in the nation. All NJ commercial insurance plans are required to cover ABA therapy with no dollar caps. This includes Horizon BCBS NJ, AmeriHealth NJ, Cigna, Aetna, and United Healthcare plans common among Hoboken's employer-covered families. NJ FamilyCare (Medicaid) also covers ABA through its managed care plans: Horizon NJ Health, Amerigroup, Aetna Better Health, and WellCare. Match Care ABA confirms your specific plan is accepted before connecting you.

NJ Early Intervention Services for Hoboken Families

NJ System Info

NJ Early Intervention System and School Transition

New Jersey's Early Intervention System (NJEIS) provides evaluations and services at no cost for children birth through age 2 with developmental concerns. Hoboken families can access NJEIS through Hudson County's early intervention coordinator. At age 3, children transition to the Hoboken Public School District for Preschool Disabilities services through the Child Study Team.

Private ABA therapy through insurance can complement NJEIS services or begin immediately while you navigate the public evaluation process — Match Care ABA can help you connect with a provider quickly.
