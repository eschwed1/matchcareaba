ABA Therapy in Trenton: What Families Need to Know

The Landscape

Medicaid-Reliant Families in a Smaller Provider Market

Trenton's families predominantly rely on NJ FamilyCare Medicaid — making it critical to find ABA providers who are contracted with the right managed care plan. NJ FamilyCare operates through multiple managed care organizations: Horizon NJ Health, Amerigroup (now Elevance Health), Aetna Better Health of NJ, and WellCare. A provider who accepts Horizon NJ Health may not accept Amerigroup, so knowing your specific plan is essential.

Match Care ABA verifies which managed care plan each provider accepts before connecting you — so families in Trenton don't waste time on providers who cannot actually serve them.

NJ Insurance Coverage for Trenton Families

New Jersey's 2009 autism insurance mandate requires all commercial plans to cover ABA therapy with no dollar caps. NJ FamilyCare (Medicaid), which covers the majority of Trenton families, also mandates ABA coverage through its managed care organizations — Horizon NJ Health, Amerigroup, Aetna Better Health of NJ, and WellCare. Match Care ABA confirms which of these plans each provider accepts before making your match.

NJ Early Intervention Services in Trenton

NJ System Info

NJEIS and Mercer County School Districts

New Jersey's Early Intervention System (NJEIS) provides free evaluations and services for Trenton children birth through age 2 with developmental delays. The Mercer County Early Intervention Coordinator handles referrals. At age 3, children transition to their local school district — Trenton Public Schools or the surrounding district — for Preschool Disabilities services through the Child Study Team.

Private ABA therapy through NJ FamilyCare can begin quickly — you do not need to wait for school district evaluation timelines. Match Care ABA can connect you with a provider who accepts your plan and is ready to serve your family now.
