Medicaid ABA Coverage Is Changing in 2026

Recent federal legislation is expected to reduce Medicaid funding significantly over the next decade. For families in Raleigh, NC relying on Medicaid to cover ABA therapy, these changes could mean reduced coverage, stricter eligibility requirements, longer waitlists, and more documentation before services begin.

Act now: Waitlists for ABA therapy were already 6–18 months long before these changes. As providers adjust to new reimbursement rates, availability for Medicaid families in Raleigh, NC may tighten further. Families who start the matching process now will be in the best position.

The Good News for Raleigh, NC Families

Under federal EPSDT rules, state Medicaid programs are still required to cover medically necessary ABA therapy for children under 21. Your child’s right to ABA therapy through Medicaid has not been eliminated. However, navigating the system is getting harder, and the families who act now will be in the best position.

EPSDT (Early and Periodic Screening, Diagnostic, and Treatment) is a federal mandate requiring state Medicaid programs to provide all medically necessary services to children under 21 — including ABA therapy when prescribed for autism spectrum disorder.

Current Access in Raleigh, NC

The Research Triangle has higher ABA provider density than most of North Carolina, but Medicaid reimbursement rates mean many providers limit the number of Medicaid slots they offer. Families in Wake, Durham, and Orange Counties typically have more options than those in surrounding rural areas, but wait times of 3 to 9 months are still common even in the Triangle. The 2026 changes could extend these further.

Insurance and Medicaid Plans

North Carolina Medicaid covers ABA therapy through Managed Care plans including AmeriHealth Caritas, Healthy Blue, UnitedHealthcare Community Plan, and WellCare. Raleigh-area families enrolled in these plans have ABA coverage for children under 21. Match Care ABA confirms your specific plan is accepted before making a match.
