NJ Insurance Coverage for Jersey City Families

New Jersey's autism insurance mandate (2009) requires all commercial plans to cover ABA therapy with no dollar caps. NJ FamilyCare Medicaid covers ABA through its managed care plans — Horizon NJ Health, Amerigroup (Elevance Health), Aetna Better Health of NJ, and WellCare. Match Care ABA specifies which managed care plan each provider accepts so you aren't connected to a provider who can't bill your specific plan.

NJ Early Intervention Services in Jersey City

NJ System Info

NJEIS and Transition to School Services

New Jersey's Early Intervention System (NJEIS) provides free evaluations and services for Jersey City children birth through age 2 with developmental delays. Families access NJEIS through Hudson County's early intervention coordinator. At age 3, children transition to Jersey City Public Schools for Preschool Disabilities services through the Child Study Team process.

Private ABA therapy through insurance can start immediately — you don't need to wait for the public system evaluation. Match Care ABA can connect you with providers quickly, even before a formal diagnosis.
