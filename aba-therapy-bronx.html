ABA Therapy in The Bronx: What Families Should Know

The Landscape

Medicaid-Centered Care in a High-Need Borough

The Bronx has the highest rates of Medicaid coverage of any NYC borough, which means Medicaid-accepted ABA providers are especially important for families here. Not all ABA practices accept every Medicaid Managed Care plan, and not all practices that appear in provider directories are actively taking new clients. This mismatch between listed availability and actual availability is one of the biggest frustrations Bronx families face.

Match Care ABA addresses this directly: we confirm that a provider accepts your specific Medicaid plan and is currently taking new families before we connect you. No more calling 15 offices and hitting dead ends.

Medicaid and Insurance Coverage in The Bronx

All NY insurance plans — including all Medicaid Managed Care plans — are required to cover ABA therapy for children with autism. Medicaid plans serving The Bronx include HealthFirst, MetroPlus, Fidelis Care, Affinity Health Plan, and Molina Healthcare. Child Health Plus also covers ABA. Commercial plans through any carrier are required to cover ABA as well. Match Care ABA verifies which specific plans a provider accepts before connecting you.

NYC Early Intervention Services in The Bronx

NYC System Info

Early Intervention, CPSE, and School-Age Services

Bronx families with children under 3 can access NYC's Early Intervention program, which provides developmental evaluations and services at no cost to families. At age 3, children transition to the Committee on Preschool Special Education (CPSE) for school-based services and related services funding.

Getting a referral to NYC Early Intervention is straightforward — your pediatrician can refer, or you can self-refer by calling 311. While school-based and EI services are a critical foundation, private ABA therapy through insurance often provides additional hours and more intensive programming that can significantly accelerate progress.
