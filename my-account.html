Diagnosis Report

Your child's autism or developmental diagnosis from a licensed provider.

A diagnosis report is required by most insurance companies to authorize ABA therapy services. It helps providers create the right treatment plan for your child.
—

Insurance Card

Front and back of your child's insurance card.

We use your insurance information to verify coverage and connect you with in-network ABA providers, so you pay as little as possible out of pocket.
—

Physician Referral

A referral letter from your child's pediatrician or physician, if available.

Some insurance plans require a physician referral before ABA services can begin. Uploading this now can help avoid delays in starting therapy.
