ABA Therapy on Long Island: Connecting Nassau and Suffolk Families with Providers

Long Island families navigating autism services face a landscape that differs significantly from New York City — different school districts, different provider networks, and suburban distances that make finding the right local provider more complex. Match Care ABA connects Nassau and Suffolk County families with verified, currently-accepting ABA therapy providers who take your insurance, at no cost to you.

ABA Therapy on Long Island: The Landscape

The Challenge

Suburban Sprawl and District-by-District Variation

Long Island spans two counties and dozens of independent school districts, each with its own special education resources and approach to autism services. This creates significant variation in what school-based support looks like from district to district — and makes private ABA therapy an important complement for many families.

Provider distribution is also uneven across the island, with more options in western Nassau County near Queens and fewer options further east into Suffolk County. Families in eastern Long Island may find that home-based ABA is more practical than clinic-based therapy. Match Care ABA helps you find providers who serve your specific community and offer the setting that works best for your family.

Insurance Coverage for ABA on Long Island

New York State law requires all insurance plans to cover ABA therapy for children with autism, including families on Long Island. Major commercial carriers serving Long Island families — Empire Blue Cross Blue Shield, Emblem Health/GHI, UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, Cigna, CDPHP, and MVP Health Care — are all required to cover ABA. Long Island families with Medicaid or Child Health Plus are also covered. Match Care ABA confirms your specific plan is accepted before making a match.

CPSE and School-Based Services on Long Island

LI School System

Working with Your School District

Long Island's many independent school districts each run their own Committee on Preschool Special Education (CPSE) and Committee on Special Education (CSE) processes. The quality and range of services varies significantly by district. Private ABA therapy through insurance is an important supplement — and sometimes the primary source of intensive support — for many Long Island families, regardless of what their school district provides.

Match Care ABA connects you with ABA providers who understand Long Island's school system and can coordinate with your child's school team when appropriate.
