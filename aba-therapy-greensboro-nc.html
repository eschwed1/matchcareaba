NC Medicaid and Insurance Coverage for ABA in Greensboro

NC General Statute 58-3-57 requires commercial insurance plans to cover ABA therapy for children with autism. NC Medicaid covers ABA through its managed care organizations including Healthy Blue, AmeriHealth Caritas NC, Carolina Complete Health, United Healthcare Community Plan, and WellCare of NC. Children with more complex behavioral health needs may be enrolled in Tailored Plans with enhanced ABA coverage. Match Care ABA verifies which managed care plan each provider accepts before making your connection.

NC Early Intervention Services in Greensboro

NC System Info

NC Infant-Toddler Program and Guilford County Schools

North Carolina's Infant-Toddler Program (ITP) provides free early intervention evaluations and services for Greensboro children birth through age 2. Guilford County's ITP coordinator handles referrals. At age 3, children transition to Guilford County Schools for Preschool Exceptional Children services through the Exceptional Children Division.

Private ABA therapy through insurance can begin right away — you do not need to wait for a school district evaluation to start services. Match Care ABA can connect you with a provider quickly, even early in the diagnosis process.
