🔍 Signs to Watch For

Your child may benefit from ABA therapy if you notice…

Every child develops differently. These signs don't mean something is wrong — they mean your child may thrive with the right specialized support.

🗣️
Delayed Speech or Language
Limited words for their age, difficulty expressing needs, or loss of language skills they previously had.
👁️
Limited Eye Contact
Avoids looking at people, doesn't respond to their name, or seems unaware of social interactions.
🔄
Repetitive Behaviors
Hand-flapping, rocking, lining up objects, or insisting on strict routines and becoming upset at changes.
🤝
Social Challenges
Difficulty playing with other children, limited interest in sharing experiences, or trouble reading social cues.
😤
Behavioral Outbursts
Intense reactions to sensory input, frequent meltdowns, or difficulty transitioning between activities.
📋
Recent Autism Diagnosis
Your child has been diagnosed with ASD and you're looking for the right next step toward therapy and support.

"If any of these sound familiar, a free consultation is the right first step."

💛 Our Story

About Match Care

Match Care was created to simplify access to Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) services for families navigating developmental care.

We connect families with independent, licensed ABA providers in their area to help reduce wait times and simplify the search process. Through provider partnerships and personalized matching, Match Care helps families move from searching to connecting — faster.

We believe access to quality care should be simple, transparent, and stress-free.

⚕️ Match Care is an independent referral service and does not provide medical advice, therapy services, or clinical treatment. All evaluations, diagnoses, and treatment decisions are made directly by licensed providers.

📍 Currently serving New York, New Jersey & North Carolina and expanding nationwide.
