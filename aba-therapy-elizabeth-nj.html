NJ Medicaid and Insurance Coverage for ABA in Elizabeth

New Jersey's Autism Insurance Reform Law (P.L. 2009, c.30) requires all commercial insurance plans to cover ABA therapy with no annual dollar caps. NJ FamilyCare Medicaid covers ABA through its managed care organizations — Horizon NJ Health, Amerigroup NJ (Elevance Health), Wellcare NJ, and Aetna Better Health of NJ. Commercial plans accepted in Elizabeth include Horizon BCBS NJ, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, and Cigna. Match Care ABA confirms exactly which managed care plan each provider accepts so you are never connected to someone who cannot bill your specific plan.

NJ Early Intervention Services in Elizabeth

NJ System Info

NJEIS and Transition to School-Based Services

New Jersey's Early Intervention System (NJEIS), administered through the Division of Developmental Disabilities, provides free evaluations and services for children birth through age 2 with developmental delays. Elizabeth families access NJEIS through Union County's early intervention coordinator. At age 3, children transition to the Elizabeth Board of Education, which provides Preschool Disabilities services and Child Study Team evaluations through its CPST program.

Private ABA therapy through insurance can begin immediately — you do not need to wait for the public system evaluation. Match Care ABA can connect you with providers quickly, even before a formal autism diagnosis is in hand.
